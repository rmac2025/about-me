My Philosophy of Customer Success

Build Trust through Curiosity and Support - Approach every customer interaction with curiosity and excitement about their work to be seen as an extension of their team. They know they can rely on me for honest, actionable support whenever possible. Customer-Centric Mindset - Focus on getting the know the customers on a personal level -- understand their motivations, needs, and objectives. Figure out how we can partner together to form a mutually beneficial relationship. Be Genuine and Transparent - Inauthentic interactions in the working world are incredibly common Prioritize Long-Term Relationships - Prioritize the customer's needs and avoid pushing anything not relevant -- knowing their business helps to maintain and nurture long-term symbiotic partnerships.

Advanced routing configuration

Vercel gives you a variety of ways to configure the routing of your website. For example, adding redirects, rewrites, or headers to your page. While you can use vercel.json to define these through JSON, you can also express your routing configuration as code using Vercel Edge Middleware.

Middleware runs before the Vercel Edge Network Cache, so you can configure routing exactly as your application needs. For example, this template uses middleware.js defined in the root of the repository to add additional security headers to the site: 

referrer-policy: origin-when-cross-origin
strict-transport-security: max-age=31536000; includeSubDomains; preload
x-content-type-options: nosniff
x-dns-prefetch-control: on
x-frame-options: DENY
